Introduction

This website shows what it's like to experience visual snow. Using either your device's camera, a video or image file, you can see some of the effects that people with visual snow syndrome experience.

Use the sliders to adjust the intensity of each effect. We'll explain more about these effects as you read down the page.

How each person experiences visual snow is incredibly subjective. This website will not be able to present a perfect simulation of what every person sees.

Visual Snow is a set of things which can make it harder to see, even when you have good eyesight. Most of the effects of visual snow can be seen even when you have your eyes closed.

Everyone can experience some or all of the effects of visual snow from time to time, but many people don't notice them, or aren't bothered by them.

Visual Snow Syndrome

When visual snow is intense enough to create visual disturbances which can affect your day-to-day life, that's when we refer to it as Visual Snow Syndrome, or VSS for short.
